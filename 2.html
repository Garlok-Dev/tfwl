I mean from the beginning that I totally support the equal, social and economic right of each person without depending on whether it is woman, man, straight, homosexual, white, black, child or adult. Of course, everything has a certain limit because, focusing on the feminist movement, it is true that women have been valued inferiorly compared to men in history, but now that we have advanced as humanity and realized our mistake we have changed and that's why I support the feminist movement, but this has gone from your hands to a point that cannot be controlled , it began as an opportunity of a more united and egalitarian society and has ended up being an excuse for when something does not work for you, you are not caught for that job but if a man, chauvinism, a man looks at me, chauvinism, a man asks you for a romantic date, violation... At what point something that promised to change history has made it worse perhaps in false allegations or perhaps when the roles were changed, maybe before the man was above the woman socially and economically but now the positions are going to be changed. Maybe if the feminist movement worked, but it worked too much to the point that now companies are afraid to hire attractive women or at least women to save themselves potential allegations of workplace harassment, a thing that started out as a solution ended up being a problem. And here my question, how far would you go to be right?
