HUMANITY AND CONSPIRACIES

Humanity has a habit of answering questions it does not understand with surreal answers, this can be seen in ancient Greece that explained everything, such as the fall of rain, with gods. Now humanity has made enough progress to know that it doesn't take a being to explain a few things. Still, the human being has not lost that custom could give you hundreds of examples such as "The Loch Ness Monster", "Bigfoot", "Reptilians", "Iluminatis", etc. Or the famous alien of Area-51, I have responded that the possibility that there is currently such a being in that military base is basically unlikely and in the remote case that an extra-terrestrial life has visited this planet there would not be so many so-called "evidence" since a being who has enough technology to travel between galaxies will have enough knowledge to know that we are idiotic beings who would rather attack those beings and lose the possibility of bringing together civilizations to move humanity forward millions of years in technology. This can be checked in every series, book, movie, etc. We assume that these beings know the concept of hate. After all, the human being is a scorpion pointing at you with his sting and the slightest movement that the interpreter as an attack will inject you, without thinking, with the poison.
